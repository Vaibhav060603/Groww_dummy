Investors are advised to invest after conducting appropriate analysis of respectives companies and not to blindly follow unfounded rumours, tips etc. Further, you are also requested to share your knowledge or evidence of systematic wrong doing, potential frauds or unethical behaviour through the anonymous portel facility provided on BSE & NSE website.



•

Awareness regarding guidelines on Margin collection

1. Stock Brokers can accept securities as margin from clients only by way of pledge in the depository system w.e.f. September 1, 2020.


2. Update your mobile number & email Id with your stock broker/depository participant and receive OTP directly from depository on your email id and/or mobile number to create pledge.


3. Pay 20% upfront margin of the transaction value to trade in cash market segment.


4. Investors may please refer to the Exchange's Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) issued vide circular reference NSE/INSP/45191 dated July 31, 202 and NSE/INSP/45534 dated August 31, 2020 and other guidelines issued from time to time in this regard.


5. Check your Securities/MF/Bonds in the consolidated account statement issued by NSDL/CDSL every month.
